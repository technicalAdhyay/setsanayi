About SET SANAYI

We turn ideas into work of art!.

Our presitigious company has been evolved from Turkish origin i.e. Ucgen. Ucgen has been found in Istanbul in the year 1947 by Ziya Sav. Primary objective of this group was to provide quality workmanship and timely completion services required for commercial, industrial and residential buildings. This group has successfully carried out many civil and electro mechanical works at Turkey, Turkmenistan, Russia and Dubai. Initially the group was actively involved in trading of construction materials.

Since 1986, after having the experience in constriction activities they solely engaged in construction and contracting and became specialised in construction buildings primarily to provide the quality workmanship and timely completion being undertaken by them. For this purpose Ucgen adopted the model of established group of contracting companies specialised in different field of activities such as Electrical by SET Electrik, Mechanical by Ucgen Mekanik. These companies, not only operating for the Ucgen projects but also working for the Electro-mechanical project of others also. And they have expanded their operations in Kazakistan, Turkmenistan, Russia and Dubai. And this expansion was not only for the purpose of expansion but to provide all round project satisfaction of the customers with high level of quality and technical perfection. As a result within a short span of time the Ucgen Group of Companies established a prominence in their field in respective countries.

Our Services

CONSTRUCTION WORK

SET.SANAYI, offers complete solution for metro rail, highways and building constructions. With experience and, International exposure, with updated technology and Skilled Staff, we are able to accurately and precisely build the project at the top quality standards, keeping time allotment in mind and deliver the best possible results.

ELECTRICAL WORK

We can maintain, install, analyse, repair equipment and troubleshoot, with the high voltage switchyards, substations, and power operations centers, that can include power transformers, load tap changers, high voltage circuit breaker, switchgear, battery and charger systems, and all and every control equipment used in substations and transmission line.

DESIGN WORK

SET Sanayi have those capabilities to have a well-designed planning and executing skills in Civil and Electrical-Mechanical designs using CAD, CAM, and 3D CAD programs. Performing computer simulation along with experimenting with virtual tests and ultimately refining the overalls of design from mid size to big size projects, across the globe.
